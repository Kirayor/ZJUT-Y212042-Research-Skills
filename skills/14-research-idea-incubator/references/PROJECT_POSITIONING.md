# Project Positioning

Use this wording when explaining the skill:

> Research Idea Incubator is not a literature retrieval agent. It is an idea validation and convergence agent. Literature search is an intermediate evidence step, while the final goal is to form a stronger research question.

## What To Emphasize

- Staged workflow, not one-shot answer generation.
- Human confirmation gates.
- Literature evidence as support, not decoration.
- Explicit uncertainty and citation-confidence labels.
- Dashboard visualization of the idea-to-question path.

## What Not To Claim

- Do not claim complete autonomous scientific discovery.
- Do not claim systematic-review completeness.
- Do not claim verified novelty without manual paper checking.
- Do not claim the skill can always download every paper automatically.
